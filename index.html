Introduction To EB-25:
In the heartwarming classroom of EB-25, the whole class is a tight-knit group of friends shining brightly like a constellation of joy. Their camaraderie is a testament to the beauty of friendship, making the classroom buzz with laughter and kindness every day. They crack jokes, help each other with assignments, and lend a listening ear, building an unbreakable bond. From sharing snacks during break time to celebrating each other's successes, they create an atmosphere of support and encouragement. These friends have become more than just classmates; they are a second family, reminding us that true friendship can blossom in unexpected places, bringing warmth and happiness to every shared moment.
Class Incharge
Prof.Shaheen Shah
In the chemistry classroom, Sir Shaheen are a personification of inspiration and warmth. With a passion for teaching and a genuine love for his students, he creates an engaging and nurturing environment that sparks curiosity and fosters growth. His enthusiasm for chemistry is infectious, making even the most complex concepts seem approachable and exciting. Sir Shaheen goes above and beyond to ensure every student understands the subject, offering patient guidance and support. Beyond academics, he genuinely cares about the well-being of his students, taking the time to listen and offer encouragement during both successes and challenges. In the hearts of his students, Sir Shaheen holds a special place as not only an educator but also a mentor and friend, leaving an indelible mark on their academic journey with kindness and dedication.
