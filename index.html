Introduction To EB-25:
In the heartwarming classroom of EB-25, the whole class is a tight-knit group of friends shining brightly like a constellation of joy. Their camaraderie is a testament to the beauty of friendship, making the classroom buzz with laughter and kindness every day. They crack jokes, help each other with assignments, and lend a listening ear, building an unbreakable bond. From sharing snacks during break time to celebrating each other's successes, they create an atmosphere of support and encouragement. These friends have become more than just classmates; they are a second family, reminding us that true friendship can blossom in unexpected places, bringing warmth and happiness to every shared moment.
